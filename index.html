How it all began

Graduating from the Polytechnic School of Chipressa, Luciano Colombo thought he would easily achieve success. However, doing what he loved caused many failures: rents were high, and there were very few clients at that time. He was stuck in debt and was already considering giving up. However, instead, Colombo sold his house, and with the proceeds he decided to expand his assortment and tried to save the store...

Our design

Everyone knows that fashion is cyclical, some things are forgotten and come back from oblivion, while some remain eternal. The wrist watches of our company from designers Dito Ramze and Achille Castiglioni demonstrate the high taste of their owner, complement his image as a connoisseur of high-quality and stylish things. A person wants his worldview and life goals to be noticeable through style and accessories. The clock is best suited for such a demonstration. Designer Denis Gvidon understands this and offers his own version of the design of a wristwatch. Denis is a supporter of avant-gardeism, which he tries to show in his works.
